Who We Are

ZEVA MEDICAL CENTRE is a reputable medical institution located in Kegogi-Marani Subcounty near the market, alongside road moving to Riragia Institute. We are committed to delivering affordable and high-quality health care to the community. At ZEVA MEDICAL CENTRE, we combine modern medical technology with compassionate care to ensure the well-being of our patients. Our dedicated team of doctors, nurses, and support staff work around the clock to provide safe, reliable, and professional health services.

Whether you're seeking preventive care or treatment for a medical condition, ZEVA MEDICAL CENTRE is here for you—caring for your health, every step of the way.
